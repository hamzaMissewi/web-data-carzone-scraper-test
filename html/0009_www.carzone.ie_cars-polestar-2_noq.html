Polestar 2 Electric Ireland

The new Polestar 2 is designed with the driver in mind. With versatile charging options & 100% Electric.

Polestar 2 Range & Performance

Polestar 2 sets the standard for progressive electric performance without frills. It curates a balance of power and range across four individual powertrains, making it the true drivers EV. Range-optimising tools like the range assistant app, pre-entry climate control, and the energy-saving heat pump extends its maximum range to up to 659 kilometers with the long range single motor.

Polestar 2 Interior

Polestar 2 offers four carefully curated interior colour schemes paired with innovative upholstery and deco materials. Discover a new form of luxury that blends comfort with a progressive, Scandinavian-inspired design language.

Made with consideration. Made to be uncompromisingly comfortable. The upholstery materials for Polestar 2 are breathable, distinctive, and pair in perfect harmony with the ergonomic seats.

Polestar 2 Technology

Intuitive software that enhances every drive. Vivid displays and crisp audio speakers that reveal new depth and clarity. The technology in Polestar 2 sets the standard for the pure and progressive electric driving experience. The Bowers & Wilkins or the Harman Kardon sound systems are optional audio configurations for Polestar 2. Each come bundled with unique features that elevate the acoustic in-car experience.

Polestar 2 Safety

With its 5-star Euro NCAP safety rating, Polestar 2 builds on decades of groundbreaking safety research and shared engineering knowledge with Volvo Cars. It comes equipped with the latest protective and preventive technology, ready to intervene when needed.
